How to perform analysis in each module?

There are six analysis modules in Spatial TME, including Spatial Structure, Spatial Gene Expression, Spatial DE Analysis, Spatial Cell Pattern, Sub-spot GEP,Cell Interaction.

1. Spatial Structure

In SpatialTME, there are two types of delineating spatial structure: morphological adjusted clusters and the malignant-boundary-non-malignant axis. All samples have the former type, while some samples with clear boundaries have both types available for selection.

2. Spatial Gene Expression

Type the gene or gene set of your interest, or select from our provided hallmark gene sets, to explore the spatial expression patterns of individual genes or gene set scores.

3. Spatial DE Analysis

In the Spatial DE Analysis module, there are two sub-modules available: DEG and Functions. Based on the two aforementioned types of spatial structure delineating, users can explore the differentially expressed genes (DEG) and the enriched functional pathways in their regions of interest.

(1) Differential expression gene analysis
(2) Functions analysis
4. Spatial Cell Pattern

In the Spatial Cell Pattern module, there are two sub-modules available: Spot composition and Cell location.

(1) Spot composition

In this sub-module, you can view two charts: the left chart displays the cellular composition of spatial spots in the region of interest or the whole sample, presented as pie charts for each spatial spots; the right one compares the cellular compositions across all regions, presented as bar charts.

(2) Cell location

You can explore the distribution of your interested cell types in a specific region or the whole sample. The left plot is a spatial feature plot that visualizes the proportion of the cell type in spatial spots using color and transparency. The right plot compares the proportions of the cell type in different regions.

5. Sub-spot GEP

This module is used for visualizing cell-specific gene expression profiles at sub-spot level. There are two sub-modules available: UMAP Plot and Gene Expression Plot.

(1) UMAP Plot

In this sub-module, you can select a specific category (such as cell type, location, or combined cell type and location) to group the data by, and visualize the distribution of sub-spots.

(2) Gene Expression Plot

Type the gene or gene set of your interest to explore the expression patterns of individual genes or gene set scores at sub-spot level.

6. Cell Interaction

In this module, you can explore the interaction weights among all cell types or choose your interested cell type as the source or target at the sub-spot level. The left plot displays the overall interaction weights between cells, while the right plot shows the interaction strengths of each ligand-receptor pair within your selected signaling pathway.

How to analyze your own data?

In the Analyze section, you can upload your data (including your ST data and scRNA-seq data for reference) in the SpatialTME. Then the analaysis results will send to your email after a period of time (about 1~4h depneds on your data size).
